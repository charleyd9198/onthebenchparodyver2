Olly Postanin

Olly grew up playing hockey in the suburbs of Calgary, in the late 90's he played for the junior B Elliot Lake Ice till they folded because they where too good.At the age of 18 Olly he was drafted by a Bengladeshi pro team where he would spend the next seven years snipping rockets.He retired a couple years ago to concentrate on teaching hockey in Bangladesh he is now back in Alberta and aims to teach hockey to beer leaguers and pro palyers

Jacob Ardown

A product of Calgary, Jacob wears with pride his 1989 Calgary Flames Stanley Cup champions jersey everywhere he goes.Jacob a late bloomer was known more for his fists then his scoring during his junior days.After his over age year in junior, Jacob being undrafted and broke went to an airport and took a flight to a random location.That random location ended up being the Virgin islands where Jacob signed a lucrative 7 year contract to play on one of the islands elite team. Now retired and back in Canada he teaches the funddies with longtime friend Olly.
